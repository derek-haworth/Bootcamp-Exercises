    Rules

  1. When the game starts, choose a character by clicking on the fighter's picture. You will fight as that character for the rest of the game.
  2. You must then defeat all of the remaining fighters.
  3. Choose an opponent by clicking on an enemy's picture
  4. Once you select an opponent, that enemy is moved to the defender area
  5. Click the attack button to damage the defender's HP, your opponent will counter and you will also lose HP
  6. You will win this game after defeating all your enemies.
